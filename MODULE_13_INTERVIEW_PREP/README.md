## Interview Day Mindset

- **Think out loud** — interviewers value your reasoning process
- **Clarify before coding** — ask about edge cases, constraints
- **State tradeoffs** — show you understand consequences of choices
- **Volunteer depth** — don't just answer the question, offer to go deeper
- **Know your own projects** — be ready to deep-dive into past work
